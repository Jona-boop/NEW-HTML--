About Raila Odinga



Raila Odinga AKA 'Jakom' anti-police brutallity is a Kenyan politician, and philanthropist who served as the first Prime Minister of Kenya.

Why I Admire Him



I admire Raila Odinga for his resilience, forgiveness, and dedication to justice. Despite spending 6 years in prison, he chose reconciliation over revenge and united a divided nation.

Key Achievements

  • First Prime Minister of Kenya
  • Led to the creation of a new constitution
  • Inspired global human rights movements
